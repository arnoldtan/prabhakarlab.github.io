Apply for Positions

SCISSOR team is looking for smart, motivated post doctoral fellows, bioinformatics specialists, research assistants. Wet lab and dry lab candidates are both welcome. Dry lab candidates should have strong mathematical intuition and programming skills, and be comfortable working in a highly collaborative multidisciplinary environment. Wet lab candidates with background in imaging-based assays and interest in cancer biology are preferred. Positions will provide an unique opportunity to develop novel assays and build elegant data analysis pipelines in the rapidly growing field of Spatial Omics. SCISSOR requires working with a multidisciplinary team that includes biologists, imaging technologists, pathologists and medical oncologists.
